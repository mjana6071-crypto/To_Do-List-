# To_Do-List

https://mjana6071-crypto.github.io/To_Do-List-/

A beautifully designed, gamified To-Do List application that turns productivity into a personal growth journey. Based on a personalized quiz, each user is paired with a unique flower. As you complete tasks, you nurture your flower—each sub goal grows a new branch, and every main-task rewards you with a star or a wish, motivating you to keep going and watch your flower bloom."

Key Features:
Personalized Flower Quiz: Discover your unique symbolic flower before you start your journey.

Gamified Task Growth: Watch your flower grow! Each completed sub task adds a new branch to your plant.

Star & Wish Rewards: Earn stars or wishes for every main-task finished, keeping you inspired and motivated to stay on track.

Progress Visualization: A beautiful, visual way to see your daily achievements bloom in real-time.

Persistent Storage: Keeps your unique flower and tasks safe, saving your progress automatically in Local Storage.

Responsive & Elegant UI: A poetic, clean, and mobile-friendly design that makes organizing your day a peaceful experience.
